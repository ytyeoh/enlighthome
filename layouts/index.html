FURNISHINGS & FITTINGS

Feel right at home with ease

Moving in and making a home is a breeze at Arica. The apartments come with everything you need to get started, from fully fitted bathrooms and built-in cabinets to essential appliances and furniture. Since design, function and durability are hallmarks of E&O’s quality standards, you can expect a living space that is tastefully fitted and furnished with quality materials and natural finishes. For instance, the master bathroom features double sinks while the living area and bedrooms showcase stylish herringbone timber floors, an E&O signature. With most of the heavy lifting taken care of, you can turn your attention to simply enjoying your new home.

APARTMENT OVERVIEW

The fundamentals of an inviting home

Experience quality and spaciousness that is synonymous with E&O. Sunlit and airy rooms created by high ceilings and generous windows, quality finishes and attention to detail are all keys to an uplifting living space. Arica’s design ethos is one of stylish simplicity that feels modern and uncluttered, with an enduring appeal that will carry you through many years to come. Arica’s two- and three- bedroom apartments come fully furnished, complete with essential fittings, built-in cabinets, appliances and furniture that has been designed and curated with care.

DESIGN & LAYOUT

Thoughtful design for optimal living

A place for everything and everything in its place, as the saying goes. A well-designed and organised home is indeed a happier one. Arica’s intuitive floor plan creates well-defined zones for activity and relaxation, socialising and privacy. The layouts make the most of every space, producing rooms and hallways that maximise functionality and flow. A must for effective organisation and keeping a clutter-free home, a dedicated utility room is provided. The yard can offer even more storage and is perfect as a seasoned cook’s wet kitchen or a laundry room.
